Interesting Ambiances at BYU-Idaho

Rick's Gardens

The Rick's Gardens at BYU-Idaho is a beutiful place to sit and study surrounded by nature, architecture, and fresh air.It's a great place to find focus, as well as interesting sights.

The Life Science Museum

The Life Science Museum, located in the Benson Building on the south side of campus, is a must see spot at BYU-Idaho. Here you can do your homework, and also learn about the many different kinds of animals that have been studies at BYU-Idaho. The Taxidermy animals are likely the closest you'll ever get to many of these animals.

The STC

The Science and Technology Center (aka the STC) is the newest building on campus. The building has such an intersting architecture and is full og interesting tech and natural light due to the big open windows on virtually every wall. It also features "study pods" and study rooms that can be reserved and used if you need your own space to focus, while still having a view of the world arounf you.
